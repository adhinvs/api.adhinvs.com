User Data Deletion

Adhin Cybertalks Automation · Last updated: 17 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of any data associated with you in connection with the application Adhin Cybertalks Automation ("the App"), operated by Adhin VS, based in Kerala, India.

Good to know: The App is designed to process comments and messages in real time and discard them once an automated response is sent. We do not maintain a persistent database of end-user message content. The only data that may persist is the access token for a connected account, which is removed when the account is disconnected.

1. If you are an account owner

To delete all data associated with your connected account:

  1. Open your Instagram or Facebook account settings.
  2. Go to Settings → Apps and Websites (or Business Integrations).
  3. Locate Adhin Cybertalks Automation and select Remove.

Removing the App revokes its access and causes the associated access token to be deleted from our system. No further data is processed for your account after removal.

2. If you are an end user

If you commented on or messaged an account that uses the App and wish to confirm deletion of any data relating to you, you can send us a request directly.

3. How to submit a deletion request

Send an email to reachadhinvs@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request and include:

  • Your Instagram or Facebook username or handle;
  • A brief description of your request.

We will process verified requests and confirm completion within 30 days. Because the App does not retain end-user message content, in most cases there is no stored personal data to delete, and we will confirm this to you.

4. What gets deleted

  • Any access token associated with a connected account;
  • Any transient data tied to your request, to the extent it exists at the time of the request.

Data already shared with Meta through normal Instagram/Facebook operation is governed by Meta's own data policies and should be managed through your Meta account settings.
